2. Amira's Happy Garden

Amira had a beautiful garden. The green grass was soft, and the colorful flowers made her smile. She loved her red tulips the most! In her garden, there was an orange tree and an apple tree. Both trees had yummy fruits growing on them. Amira also had a small avocado plant with shiny green leaves. In the middle of the garden, there was a pretty water fountain. It made a gentle splashing sound that Amira loved. Amira watered the plants, smelled the flowers, and felt so happy in her garden. It was her favorite place to play and relax.
