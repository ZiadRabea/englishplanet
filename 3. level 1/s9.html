9. Sammy the Shy Snail

Sammy the snail was very shy and liked to stay inside his shell. He watched other animals play but was too nervous to join them. One day, a butterfly landed near Sammy and asked if he wanted to play. Sammy was scared at first but decided to give it a try. To Sammy’s surprise, he had so much fun! He played with the butterfly, hopping and sliding around the garden. From that day on, Sammy was no longer shy and made lots of new friends.
