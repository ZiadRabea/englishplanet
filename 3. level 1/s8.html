8. Daisy finds a friend

Daisy the bunny loved hopping through the meadow, but she was always alone. One day, she saw a small bird sitting on a branch. Daisy asked the bird if they could play together, but the bird was shy. Daisy didn’t give up and tried again the next day. This time, the bird agreed to play with Daisy! They ran, hopped, and played games together all afternoon. From that day on, Daisy and the bird became the best of friends and played together every day.
