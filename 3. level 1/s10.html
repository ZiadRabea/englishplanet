10. The Lost Teddy Bear

Tommy loved his teddy bear very much. One day, he couldn’t find it anywhere. He searched all over the house, under the bed, and even outside. He asked everyone, but no one had seen it. Tommy felt sad without his teddy bear. He didn’t know where it could be. Then, his sister found the teddy bear hidden in the laundry basket! Tommy was so happy to hold it again.
