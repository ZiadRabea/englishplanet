7. The Sleepy Puppy

Max was a little puppy who loved to sleep. Every morning, his mom would say, “Max, it’s time to wake up!” But Max didn’t like waking up. He would close his eyes and say, “Just five more minutes!” One day, Max slept all day long. His friends, Mia the cat, Bella the bunny, and Charlie the squirrel, came to play. They called, “Max, come play with us!” But Max stayed in bed and said, “Maybe later.” When Max woke up, the sun was setting, and his friends were gone. He felt sad. The next day, Mia came to him again. She said, “Max, will you play today?” Max wagged his tail and said, “Yes, I will!” Max ran outside and played with his friends. He chased butterflies, rolled in the grass, and had so much fun.
