5. Bella's Big Balloon

Bella received a big, colorful balloon for her birthday. She was so excited and took it to the park to show her friends. The wind blew strongly, and Bella’s balloon started to float away. She ran after it, but it kept going higher into the sky. Bella was sad when the balloon got stuck in a tree. She thought of a way to get it down. With the help of her friends, Bella used a long stick to reach the balloon and finally bring it back down. She was so happy!
