6. Jenny’s New Shoes

Jenny was so happy because she got a new pair of shoes. They were bright pink with sparkly stars on them. Jenny put on her shoes and ran around the house to show them to her mom. “Look, mom! My new shoes are so pretty!” she said. Her mom smiled and said, “They look wonderful!” Jenny wore her new shoes to school the next day. All her friends said they liked her shoes, and Jenny felt proud. She couldn’t wait to wear them again.
