1. The little bird and the storm

Once upon a time, there was a little bird that lived in a small nest high in a tree. One day, a great storm came. The wind howled, and the rain poured down, but the little bird stayed inside its nest, trembling in fear. As the storm raged on, the little bird remembered what its mother had always told it: "No matter how strong the storm, always believe that the sun will come out again." The little bird took a deep breath, closed its eyes, and waited. When the storm finally passed, the sky cleared, and the sun shone brightly. The little bird opened its eyes, feeling braver than before. It flew out of its nest, ready to explore the world with newfound courage. And so, the little bird learned that after every storm, there is always a new beginning.
