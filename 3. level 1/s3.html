3. Lazy Lina

Lina loved staying in her cozy bed. Every morning, when the sun came up, her mom would call, “Lina, it’s time to wake up!” But Lina didn’t like getting up early. She would stay in bed and say, “Just five more minutes!” One day, Lina was very lazy and didn’t wake up on time. She was late for school! When she arrived, she missed her favorite storytime. Lina felt sad. The next morning, Lina remembered how she felt. She told herself, “I don’t want to miss fun things because I’m lazy!” So, she got out of bed quickly when her mom called. At school, she was on time, played with her friends, and learned many new things. Lina felt happy and proud. From that day, Lina always tried to wake up early. She learned that being on time made her day better!
