4. Timmy and the Rainbow

Timmy was a curious little boy. One day, after the rain stopped, he saw a beautiful rainbow in the sky. “I want to find the end of the rainbow!” he said. Timmy walked through the fields, over a stream, and into the woods. On his way, he met a bunny looking for carrots. “Can you help me, Timmy?” asked the bunny. Timmy stopped to help the bunny find some carrots. Next, Timmy met a bird with a hurt wing. “Can you help me, Timmy?” asked the bird. Timmy made a soft bed for the bird and gave it some water. Finally, Timmy reached the end of the rainbow. But there was no treasure. Instead, he saw the bunny and the bird. They said, “Thank you, Timmy! You are kind and helpful.” Timmy smiled and said, “Helping others is better than treasure!”
