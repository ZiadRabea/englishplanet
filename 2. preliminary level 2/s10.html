10. The Little Bird

A tiny bird was sitting on a tree branch. The bird was singing a lovely song. The trees and flowers listened to the bird. The mother bird came to the little bird. She said, "Your song is very nice!" The little bird felt very happy. It flapped its wings and flew around the tree. The little bird kept singing loudly. The other birds joined in the song. Together, they made the forest full of happy sounds.
