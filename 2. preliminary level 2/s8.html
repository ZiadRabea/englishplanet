8. The Duck and the Water

There was a yellow duck in a pond. The duck loved swimming in the water. One sunny day, the duck jumped into the pond. It splashed water everywhere. The duck said, "The water feels so cool!" It swam to the other side of the pond. It saw some fish in the water. The duck said, "Hello, little fish!" The fish swam around the duck. The duck smiled and swam happily all day.
