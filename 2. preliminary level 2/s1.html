1. The Bunny and the Carrot

There was a little bunny in the garden. The bunny was white and fluffy. It loved to hop around happily. One day, the bunny saw a big orange carrot. The carrot was under a tall green tree. The bunny hopped to the carrot. It picked up the carrot with its little paws. The bunny took a big bite of the carrot. It chewed and said, "Yummy, this is so tasty!" The bunny felt very happy and hopped away with the carrot.
