3. The sparrow and the Nest

A little sparrow was looking for a place to live. It flew high in the sky and found a tree. The sparrow decided to build a nest on a strong branch. It worked hard and made the nest with sticks and leaves. The sparrow laid three eggs in the nest. It sat on the eggs to keep them warm. After a few days, the eggs began to crack. Little baby birds came out. The sparrow was very happy to see her chicks. She fed them worms and kept them safe in the nest.
