5. The Butterfly and the Flowers

A colorful butterfly was flying in the garden. It saw many pretty flowers. The butterfly landed on a red flower. The flower smelled very nice. The butterfly said, "I love this flower!" Then it flew to a yellow flower. The yellow flower was big and bright. The butterfly sat there for a while. It flapped its wings happily. The butterfly loved flying and visiting flowers every day.
