2.The Cat and the Ball

There was a playful little cat. It loved to run and jump all day. One morning, the cat found a shiny ball. The ball was red and very small. The cat touched the ball with its paw. The ball rolled across the floor. The cat ran after it quickly. It pushed the ball again and again. The ball bounced under the chair. The cat smiled and kept playing with the ball all day.
