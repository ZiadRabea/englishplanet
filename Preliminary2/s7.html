7. The Dog and the Bone

A little brown dog was walking in the garden. It saw a big bone under a tree. The dog picked up the bone with its mouth. It wagged its tail happily. The dog ran to a quiet corner. It sat down and started chewing the bone. The bone was hard but tasty. The dog said, "This is the best bone ever!" It chewed and chewed until it was tired. The dog lay down with the bone and fell asleep.
