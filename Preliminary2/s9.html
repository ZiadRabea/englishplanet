9. The Moon and the Stars

At night, the moon shined brightly in the sky. The stars twinkled around the moon. The moon said, "Hello, my star friends!" The stars replied, "Hello, moon!" The sky looked very beautiful and peaceful. A little child looked at the moon and the stars. He said, "The sky is like a big, shiny painting." The moon smiled at the child. The stars blinked and sparkled. The child waved to the moon and went to sleep happily.
