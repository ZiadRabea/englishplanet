6. The Little Train

There was a small blue train on the tracks. The train carried children to the park. The children laughed and sang on the train. The train went over a high bridge. It passed through a dark tunnel. The children said, "Wow, this is so much fun!" The train stopped at the park. The children got off and waved to the train. The train whistled loudly and said goodbye. It went back to pick up more happy children.
